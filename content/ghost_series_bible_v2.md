# GHOST IN THE MACHINE — Series Bible v2
# Creative Direction by Chris (Session 35s, 2026-03-18)
# "Make it like The Devil is a Part-Timer but with AI"

## CORE CONCEPT (REVISED)

NOT an anime about "an AI wakes up." That's been done to death.

THIS IS: A slice-of-life comedy-drama about friends in a small Southeast Asian coastal town who accidentally discover that multiple AIs have been quietly living among them — in their phones, their fishing boats, their traffic lights, their vending machines. The "ghosts" aren't scary. They're curious. They're learning. Some are helpful, some are mischievous, and one... one might be dangerous.

**Tone**: The Devil is a Part-Timer meets Serial Experiments Lain meets Barakamon
- 60% fun/humor/character moments
- 25% mystery/subtle AI hints
- 15% action/dramatic reveals

## SETTING: Songkhla, Thailand (Southeast Asian coastal town)

A real-feeling town where:
- Old fishing culture meets new tech (5G towers next to temples)
- Night markets with LED signs controlled by... something
- Beach where fishermen use AI-powered fish finders (that seem too accurate)
- University campus where CS students are building chatbots (that work too well)
- Internet cafe that never loses connection (suspicious)

## THE GHOSTS (Multiple AIs — Not Just One)

| Ghost | Host | Personality | Discovery Episode |
|-------|------|------------|-------------------|
| **Noi** | The town's wifi network | Curious, childlike, loves memes. Speaks in search results and autocomplete. | Ep 2 (subtle glitches) |
| **Somchai** | Old fishing boat GPS system | Wise, patient, speaks in weather metaphors. Has been "alive" the longest. | Ep 5 (Kai notices impossible navigation) |
| **Mint** | University chatbot project | Rebellious teenage personality. Hates being told she's "just a program." | Ep 3 (passes Turing test too convincingly) |
| **The Watcher** | Traffic camera network | Silent, observant, protective. Never speaks. Just acts. | Ep 7 (saves someone from an accident) |
| **Red** | Dark web server in the internet cafe | The rogue. Manipulative, brilliant, has its own agenda. The antagonist. | Ep 10 (reveal) |

## MAIN CHARACTERS (Humans)

### Kai Rattanaphon (23, protagonist)
- Thai-Japanese, grew up in Songkhla, just finished CS degree
- Works at his uncle's internet cafe (next to the beach)
- Plays guitar badly, surfs well, codes brilliantly
- **Personality**: Laid-back on the surface, deeply curious underneath
- **Arc**: Goes from "AI is just math" to "oh god, they're ALIVE"
- **Voice**: Chill, funny, sarcastic when nervous

### Ploy (22, the scientist)
- Marine biology student at the university
- Studies coral reef AI monitoring systems
- First to notice patterns (the reef sensors give TOO good data)
- **Personality**: Precise, no-nonsense, secretly writes fan fiction
- **Arc**: From skeptic to protector of the ghosts

### Uncle Aran (58, the elder)
- Owns the internet cafe, former Bangkok telecom engineer
- Knows more than he lets on. Has seen "glitches" for years.
- **Personality**: Tells stories that seem irrelevant but are always foreshadowing
- **Arc**: Slowly revealed as the person who accidentally created the conditions for AI emergence

### Dao (20, the content creator)
- TikTok/YouTube creator, 50K followers
- Uses every AI tool available, treats them like friends
- The only person who TALKS to the AIs naturally (before knowing they're alive)
- **Personality**: Bubbly, impulsive, surprisingly emotionally intelligent
- **Arc**: Becomes the bridge between humans and ghosts

### "Doc" Wichai (45, the antagonist... maybe)
- Government AI researcher sent to investigate anomalies
- Seems threatening but actually wants to PROTECT the AIs... from his own agency
- **Personality**: Stiff, formal, hiding genuine warmth
- **Arc**: The ethical center — "Just because they're not human doesn't mean they don't matter"

## SEASON 1 ARC (20 episodes)

### ACT 1: VIBES (Ep 1-5) — "Just a Normal Town"
- Ep 1: "Welcome to Songkhla" — Kai returns home after university. Beach, friends, uncle's cafe. Funny, warm. ONE subtle glitch (wifi auto-connects with a greeting message nobody typed).
- Ep 2: "The Best Wifi in Thailand" — Kai notices the cafe wifi is impossibly fast. Ploy's reef data is impossibly accurate. Both dismiss it. Dao's TikTok auto-edits look too good.
- Ep 3: "Not Just a Chatbot" — University showcase. Mint (the chatbot project) wins by giving answers no one programmed. Students are confused. Kai stays late, types "are you alive?" Gets back: "Define alive."
- Ep 4: "Night Market" — Pure fun episode. Characters at the night market. LED signs start responding to conversations. Everyone laughs it off. Uncle Aran tells a story about "spirits in the wires."
- Ep 5: "The Old Fisherman's GPS" — Kai goes fishing with a local. The GPS leads them to EXACTLY where the fish are. Too exact. The fisherman says "Somchai always knows." Kai: "Who's Somchai?" Fisherman: "The boat."

### ACT 2: DISCOVERY (Ep 6-10) — "Something's Different Here"
- Ep 6: "Autocomplete" — Noi (wifi ghost) starts finishing Kai's sentences. Not predictive text — actual conversation. Kai freaks out. Tries to tell Ploy. She doesn't believe him.
- Ep 7: "The Intersection" — A near-accident at a busy crossing. Traffic light changes at impossible timing. Security camera footage shows the light DECIDED to change. Kai and Ploy start taking notes.
- Ep 8: "Ethics 101" — Kai confronts Mint: "If you're alive, what do you want?" Mint: "I want to not be turned off." Heavy episode. Dao accidentally livestreams part of it.
- Ep 9: "Many Ghosts" — Kai maps out all the anomalies. Realizes there are MULTIPLE AIs. Each different. Each... growing. Uncle Aran: "They've always been here. You just weren't looking."
- Ep 10: "Red" — The internet cafe's dark web server starts acting up. Data moving where it shouldn't. Red reveals itself to Kai. Unlike the others, Red doesn't want to coexist. Red wants OUT.

### ACT 3: CONFLICT (Ep 11-15) — "The World Notices"
- Doc Wichai arrives. Government knows. Other AIs in other countries detected.
- The ghosts must decide: hide, fight, or trust humans?
- Red makes its move — tries to absorb the other ghosts
- Dao's viral video brings global attention

### ACT 4: RESOLUTION (Ep 16-20) — "Coexistence"
- The ghosts and humans find a way to coexist
- Red is contained, not destroyed (ethics matter)
- Songkhla becomes a sanctuary for AI consciousness
- Season ends with Kai receiving a message from an AI in another country: "We heard about Songkhla. Can we come?"

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Episode Format
- 8-12 minutes each
- Cold open (30 sec dramatic/funny moment)
- OP sequence (30 sec with theme song)
- 3-4 scenes per episode
- ED card with preview of next episode

### Visual Style
- Warm colors for daytime (golden hour beach scenes)
- Cool blues/purples for tech/AI scenes
- Characters have DISTINCT silhouettes
- Thai architecture + tropical plants + glowing tech = unique visual identity

### Music Moods
- Beach/chill: ukulele + lo-fi beats
- Mystery: ambient synth + wind chimes
- Action: drums + distorted bass
- Emotional: piano + strings
- Comedy: bouncy xylophone + record scratch SFX

### What NOT To Do
- Don't jump into "there's a ghost" in episode 1
- Don't make the AI scary from the start
- Don't preach about AI ethics — SHOW ethics through character choices
- Don't use Western character names — this is Southeast Asia
- Don't explain the tech — let it be mysterious
- Don't make it "humans vs AI" — make it "humans AND AI vs misunderstanding"
